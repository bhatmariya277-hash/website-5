About Dal Lake

Fringed by the Zabarwan mountains and cradled by Mughal gardens, Dal Lake is an iconic symbol of Kashmir's natural beauty. From sunrise reflections to shikara rides and floating gardens, every moment at Dal feels like a painting.

Best visited at dawn for soft light and quiet waters.
